What I Do

Help Others Do More

Everything I do is aimed at helping others do more. It is my purpose and I fulfil this through looking for improvement opportunities and ways to make best use of data. Whether I am improving a process, performing a piece of analysis and packaging it up, learning a new tool or technique, or training someone on tools and techniques in my repertoire, I'm always looking to help others do more.

Make The Complex Simple

I appreciate that most people are not numbers people. They baulk at the word "spreadsheet", so that is where I come in. Being an experienced analyst and advisor I can take a business problem and make use of data to help find a solution. From understanding where data has come from, how fit-for-purpose it is, and turning it into a format that is digestable to the "not-another-spreadsheet" people, this is what I do.

  Analysis

I analyse, interpret, and present findings using a range of techniques to suit the situation. My experience has allowed me to work in many different environments, so I appreciate the need for tailoring solutions to fit.

  Visualisation

I am experienced with visualisation methods that include Excel charts, PowerPoint presentations, A3 One-pagers, and dashboarding in tools like Power BI. My ability to pick up new tools and skillsets allows me to pivot to whatever options are available to me and make the most of the available toolset.

  Collaboration

My preference is to collaborate wherever possible as I believe it will always provide a better result. I enjoy working with others, so that I can learn from them and they can learn from me. Whether it is business stakeholders, senior executives, or my peers, I am comfortable with managing relationships to enhance collorative opportunities.

  Improvement

Always on the lookout for opportunities to improve. Improve the way things work, improve myself or others, improve how things will be done next time. I bring a mindset of challenging what is to shine a light on what could be... and when the conditions are right, bring those ideas to fruition.
